ZOMBIE

  • 1.5 oz Jamaican rum
  • 1.5 oz gold Puerto Rican rum
  • 1 oz 151-proof Demerara rum
  • 1/8 tsp Herbsaint or Pernod
  • 1/2 oz falernum
  • 1/2 oz Donn’s Mix (see below)
  • 1 tsp grenadine
  • dash Angostura bitters
  • 6 oz crushed ice

Put everything in blender, saving the ice for last. Blend at high speed for no more than 5 seconds. Pour into a glass and add ice cubes to fill. Garnish.

PINA COLADA

  • 1.5 oz coconut cream
  • 1.5 oz pineapple juice
  • 1 oz coconut rum
  • splash of coconut milk
  • pineapple wedge, for garnish

Combine all the ingredients in a blender cup. Add 1 cup of ice. Blend on High until smooth. Pour contents into a tiki glass or tall glass. Garnish with the pineapple wedge.

(thanks epicurious!)

TRADER VIC'S ORIGINAL MAI TAI

  • 2 oz aged rum
  • 3/4 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice, juiced lime half reserved
  • 1/2 ounce orange curaçao
  • 1/4 ounce rich simple syrup
  • 1/4 ounce orgeat
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 1 mint sprig, for garnish

Combine all ingredients except the mint sprig in a cocktail shaker, shake vigorously, and pour the entire contents into a double Old Fashioned glass.

Garnish with the juiced lime half and a mint sprig.

SINGAPORE SLING

  • 1.25 oz Bombay Sapphire Gin
  • Bénédictine
  • Cherry brandy
  • Club soda
  • Lime wedge

Pour the gin, Bénédictine and cherry brandy into a sling or highball glass. Fill with ice and top with club soda. Squeeze a lime wedge over the drink, drop it into the glass and stir. Garnish with a cherry.

PINEAPPLE HOP

  • 1 medium fresh pineapple, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1.5 cups granulated sugar
  • 1.5 cups apple cider vinegar
  • 5 oz white rum
  • 1.25 oz orgeat, such as Small Hand Foods
  • 20 oz India Pale Ale, such as Stone IPA

For the Pineapple Shrub: Place the pineapple cubes in a clean, sterilized wide-mouth container. Cover with sugar and lightly crush the pineapples using a potato masher or muddler. Cover and refrigerate 10 hours. Strain fruit (discard or save for another use) and add apple cider vinegar to the liquid. Seal with lid and refrigerate for two weeks, shaking the mixture every other day. After two weeks, strain into a clean airtight container—you'll have about 3 cups—and refrigerate. Strained shrub keeps up to 6 months.

For the Cocktails: In a pitcher, mix together white rum, 5 oz of Pineapple Shrub, and orgeat. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving, or mix and refrigerate up to 24 hours in advance. When ready to serve, add beer to the pitcher and stir gently. Serve cocktails in ice-filled highball glasses.

(thanks serious eats!)

HEMINGWAY DAQUIRI

  • serves 2
  • 2 oz white rum
  • .5 oz Maraschino liqueur
  • .75 oz lime juice
  • ice (for shaking)
  • lime wheels (for garnish)

Pour the rum, lime juice, grapefruit juice and maraschino liqueur in a cocktail shaker. Shake with ice, strain, and serve with a slice of lime for garnish.

TOP NOTCH VOLCANO

  • serves 4
  • 4 oz fresh lime juice
  • 4 oz fresh pineapple juice
  • 1 oz passionfruit nectar
  • 1 oz Luxardo Maraschino liqueur
  • 4 oz aged white rum
  • 4 oz dark rum
  • 4 oz. Demerara simple syrup
  • Grated cinnamon (optional, for sparks)
  • Grated nutmeg (optional, for sparks)
  • Tiki mugs or very long straws for serving

Combine all ingredients except spices in a pitcher over ice. Stir until well mixed and chilled. Strain into the liquid portion of the volcano bowl and add large blocks of fresh ice. The size of the cubes is important, so that they keep the drink cold and melt slowly.


Place toast crumbs soaked in lemon extract in the volcano reservoir of the bowl. Light the toast. Place long straws in bowl, or ladle drink into individual tiki mugs.


Optional: For added flare, combine the cinnamon and nutmeg in a shaker. Shake sprinkles over the flame to create sparks.


(thanks robb vices!)


PLANTER'S PUNCH

  • serves 4
  • 1.25 oz Margaritaville® Silver Rum
  • .75 oz Margaritaville® Dark Rum
  • 1 oz sour mix
  • 1 oz orange juice
  • 1 oz pineapple juice

Combine ingredients in a shaker except for the dark rum. Shake and pour into a glass with ice. Float dark rum. Garnish with an orange wedge and cherry.
